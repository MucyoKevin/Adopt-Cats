About Us

AdoptsCats: Finding Your Purrfect Match!

AdoptsCats is a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting loving homes with wonderful cats in need.
We were founded in 2015 by a group of passionate cat lovers who saw a growing number of homeless felines in our community.

Since then, we've facilitated the adoption of over 2,500 cats, transforming countless lives â€“ both feline and human!

Our Mission

  1. Rescue and Care: We take in stray, abandoned, and surrendered cats, providing them with a safe haven, veterinary care, and plenty of love and attention.
  2. Find Forever Homes: We believe every cat deserves a loving forever home. We work tirelessly to match each cat's unique personality with the perfect adopter.
  3. Educate and Advocate: We promote responsible cat ownership through educational resources and community outreach programs. We strive to create a world where all cats are treated with compassion and respect.

Our Approach

  1. Detailed Cat Profiles: Each cat on our website has a personalized profile with photos, a detailed description of their personality and needs, and any veterinary information.
  2. Adoption Counseling:Our experienced adoption counselors are here to guide you every step of the way, from browsing profiles to understanding cat behavior and helping you find the perfect feline friend.
  3. Post-Adoption Support:We don't stop caring once the adoption is complete. We offer ongoing support to ensure a smooth transition for both you and your new cat.

Join the AdoptsCats Family

Whether you're looking to adopt a cuddly lap cat, a playful kitten, or a majestic senior feline, we're confident we have the purrfect match waiting for you.

Browse our adoptable cats today, or consider volunteering your time or donating to help us continue our lifesaving work.

Together, we can make a difference in the lives of countless cats.
