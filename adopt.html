Adopt A Cat

Find Your Furever Friend at AdoptsCats!

At AdoptsCats, we know finding the perfect feline companion can be an exciting and heartwarming experience. We're here to guide you through the adoption process every step of the way, ensuring a smooth transition for both you and your new furry friend.

Ready to Adopt? Here's How it Works:

  1. Browse Our Adoptable Cats: Start your journey by browsing our extensive database of adoptable cats! Each profile features captivating photos, detailed descriptions of their personality and needs (playful, cuddly, independent, etc.), age, breed (if known), and any veterinary information. You can filter your search by age, coat type, special needs, and even energy level to find a cat that best suits your lifestyle.
  2. Find Your Perfect Match: Don't feel overwhelmed by the adorable faces! Let our profiles guide you. Read through the descriptions and take note of cats who seem like a good fit for your home environment and personality.
  3. Submit an Adoption Inquiry: Once you've identified a few potential feline companions, click the "Inquire About Adoption" button on their profile. This will take you to a quick form where you can provide some basic information about yourself, your living situation, and your experience with cats.
  4. Connect with an Adoption Counselor: A dedicated adoption counselor will review your inquiry and contact you within 24-48 hours to discuss your preferences and answer any questions you may have about the adoption process or specific cats you're interested in.
  5. Schedule a Meet & Greet: If there seems to be a good match, our counselor will help you schedule a meet-and-greet at our shelter or a foster home (depending on the cat's situation). This is your chance to interact with the cat in a relaxed environment and see if there's a spark!
  6. Finalize the Adoption: Once you've found your purrfect match, our adoption counselor will guide you through the final steps, including reviewing the adoption contract, discussing any adoption fees (which cover the cost of vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping), and finalizing paperwork.
  7. Welcome Home Your New Family Member!: We'll provide you with a comprehensive adoption packet filled with information on cat care, nutrition, behavior training, and local vet recommendations to ensure a smooth transition for your new feline friend.
